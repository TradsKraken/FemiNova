Karahasan / Pang-aabuso

Violence Prevention & Support

Breaking the silence on gender-based violence and providing comprehensive support for survivors.

Our Mission

We are committed to ending all forms of violence against women through education, advocacy, and direct support services. Our campaign addresses domestic violence, sexual assault, harassment, and other forms of gender-based abuse.

  • • 24/7 crisis hotline and support
  • • Legal advocacy and assistance
  • • Safe housing and shelter services
  • • Counseling and therapy programs

Kwentong Balita

Stories That Matter

Sharing untold stories and experiences that matter to women in our communities.

About Kwentong Balita

Every woman has a story worth telling. Kwentong Balita (News Stories) is our platform for amplifying the voices of women whose experiences often go unheard in mainstream media.

We share stories of resilience, triumph, struggle, and hope from women across different walks of life, creating a tapestry of experiences that connects us all.

Story Categories:

  • • Personal journeys and transformations
  • • Professional achievements and challenges
  • • Community leadership and activism
  • • Cultural preservation and innovation

Testimonyal

Voices of Strength

Powerful testimonies from women who have overcome challenges and found strength.

The Power of Testimony

Testimonyal is our collection of powerful firsthand accounts from women who have faced adversity and emerged stronger. These testimonies serve as beacons of hope and sources of inspiration for others facing similar challenges.

Through video, audio, and written testimonies, we create a safe space where women can share their truth and connect with others who understand their journey.

Types of Testimonies:

  • • Overcoming domestic violence
  • • Mental health recovery journeys
  • • Career transformation stories
  • • Educational achievement against odds
  • • Health and wellness breakthroughs
